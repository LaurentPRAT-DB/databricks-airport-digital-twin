# UX Review — Airport Digital Twin (2026-03-21)

Deployed build #274 via DABs, reviewed on https://airport-digital-twin-dev-7474645572615955.aws.databricksapps.com

## Executive Summary

The app works well overall: airport geometry renders correctly, flight data is realistic, 3D view is functional, and airport switching works across 29 cached airports. However, there are several issues ranging from critical bugs to polish opportunities.

**Severity levels:** P0 = blocks demo, P1 = noticeable/confusing, P2 = polish

---

## P0 — Critical Bugs

### 1. Gate 56 stacking: 12 flights assigned to same gate (RJTT)
- **What:** 12 flights (ANA9110, JAL3112, ANA660, ANA5657, ANA2010, ANA6850, JAL5384, ANA9249, ANA7755, JAL5876, JAL4460, SKY3091) all assigned to gate 56 simultaneously.
- **Impact:** All 12 aircraft render at the exact same position on the map — looks like a single icon. Completely breaks the realism of gate assignments.
- **Root cause likely:** Gate assignment overflow — when all 87 gates are occupied and new flights need a gate, they may all fall back to the same default gate.
- **Evidence:** API confirmed via `/api/flights?include_gates=true` — `assigned_gate: "56"` for all 12.
- **KJFK comparison:** JFK did NOT show this issue — gate assignments were properly distributed (A3, A6, B24, B30, etc.). This may be RJTT-specific.

### 2. 36 flights with no gate assignment (RJTT)
- **What:** 36 out of 100 flights have no `assigned_gate` value.
- **Impact:** These flights appear as "no gate" on the map — they render at computed positions that may not align with actual gate infrastructure. Some appear on taxiways or aprons instead of at gates.
- **Combined with #1:** Only 52 flights have unique gate assignments (out of 87 available gates), 12 share gate 56, 36 have none. Gate allocator appears broken.

## P1 — Noticeable Issues

### 3. Airport switch takes ~35 seconds perceived time
- **What:** Switching from RJTT to KJFK took 34.8 seconds wall clock.
- **Backend timing:** Lakebase cache hit in 0.3s for KJFK — the backend is fast.
- **Bottleneck:** The remaining ~34.5s is spent on: synthetic flight generation, schedule creation, gate assignment, turnaround initialization, and frontend re-rendering.
- **Impact:** Users may think the app is frozen. The progress overlay helps but 35s is too long for a demo.
- **Recommendation:** Pre-generate synthetic schedules for cached airports, or run generation in a background thread with progressive loading (show map immediately, populate flights as they arrive).

### 4. Aircraft silhouettes hard to see on satellite view
- **What:** When satellite tiles are enabled, the dark aircraft silhouettes blend into the satellite imagery and are nearly invisible.
- **Recommendation:** Add a white outline/halo around aircraft silhouettes on satellite view, or increase silhouette brightness.

### 5. Aircraft stopped on taxiway instead of reaching gate
- **What:** ANA9475 was observed taxiing at 25kts (heading 278°/325°), then stopped at 0kts on a taxiway intersection — not at a gate.
- **Possible cause:** Taxi animation runs for a fixed duration without pathfinding to the actual gate location. When the "taxi" phase timer expires, the aircraft just stops wherever it is.
- **Impact:** Aircraft "parked" mid-taxiway looks unrealistic.

### 6. All delay predictions show identical values
- **What:** Every flight checked showed "Slight Delay +13m 85%" or similar values.
- **Impact:** Looks like a placeholder rather than a real prediction. The ML model may not be differentiating between flights sufficiently.

### 7. Turnaround progress shows "Refueling" even for stopped/idle aircraft
- **What:** ANA9475 showed "Refueling 100%" with turnaround progress even though it had stopped on a taxiway, not at a gate.
- **Impact:** Turnaround should not start until aircraft is actually docked at a gate.

## P2 — Polish & Enhancements

### 8. No visible progress indicator during RJTT→KJFK switch
- **What:** During the 35-second switch, there was no loading spinner visible (the map just showed the old airport briefly, then switched). The progress overlay documented in the codebase may not have triggered properly.
- **Recommendation:** Ensure the progress overlay is consistently shown during all airport switches.

### 9. Flight list doesn't indicate flight phase visually
- **What:** Flights show "GND", "CRZ", "DSC" as small text badges, but the list doesn't color-code or group them meaningfully.
- **Recommendation:** Color-code the GND/CRZ/DSC/CLB badges to match the legend colors in the header (green/blue/orange/red).

### 10. 9 terminal tabs but most empty at RJTT
- **What:** RJTT shows Terminal 1-9, but Terminal 3 (1 gate), Terminal 8 (1 gate), Terminal 9 (1 gate) are almost empty. Some have 0 aircraft.
- **Note:** This reflects real OSM data and isn't necessarily wrong, but small terminals with 0-1 gates look odd.

### 11. Map doesn't auto-center on selected flight
- **What:** When clicking a flight in the left panel, the map doesn't pan to center on that flight. You have to manually find it on the map.
- **Recommendation:** Auto-pan/zoom to selected flight when clicking from the flight list.

### 12. 2D/3D view state not synchronized
- **What:** Selection and zoom level not shared between 2D and 3D views (already a known issue per MEMORY.md).

### 13. 3D building models are flat boxes
- **What:** Terminal buildings in 3D are rendered as flat-roofed grey boxes. While functionally fine, they lack visual appeal.
- **Recommendation:** Low priority, but curved roofs or textured facades would improve realism.

### 14. Weather display shows "Loading weather..." briefly
- **What:** On initial load, the weather badge shows "Loading weather..." for a moment before showing actual data.
- **Minor:** Not a bug, just a loading state that could be improved with a skeleton placeholder.

## Positive Observations

1. **Airport selector UX is excellent** — 29 airports organized by region (Americas, Europe, Middle East, Asia-Pacific, Africa) with ICAO/IATA codes, names, cities. Custom ICAO input available. "All 29 airports cached" status indicator.
2. **Flight data is realistic for each airport** — RJTT shows ANA/JAL/KAL/SKY flights, KJFK shows AAL/DAL/JBU/KLM/DLH flights. Correct airline mixes per airport.
3. **Weather is airport-specific** — RJTT showed 20°C 188@3kt, KJFK showed 12°C 270@0kt. Realistic.
4. **3D view works well** — Aircraft models render properly, buildings visible, top-down view useful for comparison with 2D.
5. **Turnaround detail panel is rich** — Shows boarding %, GSE equipment (fuel truck, ground power), baggage status, connecting bags at risk.
6. **Trajectory feature works** — Shows departure direction correctly (HND→CTS trajectory pointed northeast toward Sapporo).
7. **Gate status panel is informative** — Terminal breakdown with free/used counts, congestion levels.
8. **Backend performance is excellent** — Lakebase cache hits in 0.3-0.6s.
9. **No console errors** — Clean console (only 1 minor 404).
10. **WebSocket connection stable** — "Connected" status maintained throughout the session.

### New Issues Found

#### P1: FIDS Origins Are Random — Not Realistic Route Networks
The schedule generator picks random IATA codes globally rather than airline-realistic routes:

**KSFO examples:**
- `THY2940 CVG→SFO` — Turkish Airlines flying domestic US
- `DLH2697 SAT→SFO` — Lufthansa flying San Antonio→SFO
- `UAL746 RMO→SFO` — "RMO" is not a standard commercial airport
- `UAL1133 LTO→SFO` — Loreto, Mexico: not a real United route

**RJTT examples:**
- `SIA297 PPS→HND` — Singapore Airlines from Puerto Princesa
- `THA687 GOX→HND` — Thai from Goa (not a real route)
- `SIA404 MDW→HND` — Singapore Airlines from Chicago Midway

**EGLL examples:**
- `SAS2288 MJI→LHR` — SAS from Mitiga, Libya
- `EZY1625 LTN→LHR` — easyJet Luton→Heathrow (30-mile bus ride)
- `AUA2425 ALB→LHR` — Austrian from Albany, NY

**Impact:** Anyone familiar with aviation will immediately notice absurd routes. This is the most visible data quality issue in the FIDS.

**Fix approach:** Create per-airline route tables. Each airline should only be assigned to realistic origin/destination airports they actually serve.

#### P2: FIDS Gate Collision in Future Schedule
- RJTT: Gate "110" appears 3× simultaneously in FIDS arrivals
- Gate "20" appears 2× simultaneously
- The future schedule generator doesn't check gate occupancy

#### P2: Low Gate Utilization
- KSFO: 107 gates available, only 5-18 occupied (5-17%)
- Real SFO would have 30-60% utilization during peak hours

#### P3: Taxi-Out Aircraft Stuck at speed=0
- 5 of 5 taxi_out aircraft had velocity=0 in snapshot
- Heading=0° for 1 taxiing aircraft (default, not computed)

#### P3: E175 Regional Jet on Intercontinental Route
- `ASA4822 (E175) SFO→AMS` — Alaska E175 to Amsterdam
- Regional jets should be capped at ~2000nm routes
